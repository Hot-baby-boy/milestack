The workflow

From invite to payout, in six steps

Every workspace follows the same trusted sequence, so both sides always know exactly where the money and the work stand.

01

Invite Your Client

Send a workspace link by email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or any channel you already use. No marketplace account required on either side.

02

Create Milestones

Break the project into clear, priced milestones with deadlines, deliverables, and scope, set once, visible to both sides.

03

Client Funds Project

Your client deposits the milestone amount plus a 10% Project Protection Fee into escrow before work begins. You'll receive the full milestone amount, the fee is what the client pays for protection, not a cut of your earnings.

04

Deliver Work

Upload files, share progress, and message your client inside the workspace, one shared record for the whole project.

05

Client Approves

Your client reviews the deliverable against the milestone and approves it, or requests changes with specific feedback.

06

Payment Released

Funds release straight to your account the moment a milestone is approved. No invoices to chase, no waiting on "payment sent."

Everything the relationship needs

Built for work between two people who already trust each other, mostly

Milestack handles the parts that get awkward: money, proof of delivery, and what happens when something goes wrong.

Escrow Payments

Clients fund milestones through regulated payment infrastructure, paying the project amount plus a 10% Project Protection Fee. Freelancers receive 100% of the agreed amount, Milestack never takes a cut of your earnings, and never takes custody of the money itself.

Freelancer keeps 100% Held until approval Fee paid by client

Milestone Tracking

Every deliverable has a status (draft, funded, in progress, submitted, approved, or released) visible to both sides at all times.

Contracts & Agreements

Generate a scope-of-work agreement from your milestones and have both parties sign before any money moves.

Project Chat

One thread per workspace, attached to the milestone it's about, no more digging through WhatsApp for the brief.

File Sharing

Drafts, revisions, and final deliverables live inside the milestone they belong to, version by version.

Identity Verification

Government ID, selfie liveness check, and address verification keep every workspace tied to a real, accountable person.

Dispute Resolution

If a milestone is contested, either side can open a case with evidence and chat history for a binding review.

Portfolio Profiles

A public profile with verified work history, completed projects, and client reviews, built from real, funded work.
